About Us

The perfect spot for book lovers....

Bookie store was created for readers of every generation to find their little paradise, accessible from anywhere in the world at any time. Have you ever experienced the frustration of readers all around the world when you want to read a book, and cannot find it at your local book store? or when you cannot afford the new edition of your favorite book hard cover, and you stand in front of the store wondering if you'll ever get to read it?

With bookie store, this is all over. Bookie store provides an easy and affordable way of renting books online. This way, readers can save money while still having the pleasure of holding a real life book and reading it.
